Introduction

A gasket is a sealing element used to prevent leaks of fluids, gases, or particles between two surfaces. Made from materials like rubber, metal, or plastic, it ensures a tight, secure fit in various systems.

ZNI Gaskets Products

We offer three main types of gaskets:
1. Non-Metallic – For general-purpose sealing using rubber or fiber.
2. Semi-Metallic – A mix of metal and non-metal for improved performance.
3. Metallic – All-metal gaskets for high-pressure, high-temperature conditions.
